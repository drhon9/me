Who is DrHong

DrHong is going.. Aug 2, 2019

Euy Shick Hong(DrHong), (born 1980, Republic of Korea) is a computer musician, software engineer, Jazz Saxophonist. He completed a D.M.A (Doctor of Musical Arts) in Computer Music and Composition at MARTE(The Laboratory of Musical Arts and Technology) in Dongguk University.

Doctoral dissertation is "A study of Interactive Web Application through Web-based Audio Library Development" by Web technology such as Javascript, Web Audio API, Node.js in 2019.

In MARTE, he studied sound synthesis, sound analysis, interactive performance, HCI, physical computing with arduino, programming and published papers, books while playing the saxophone for concerts and albums.

Now He works as a software engineer in IT company (Javascript, MySQL, React, Node.js..), and CTO at ICMT(institute of computer music and technology) playing the saxophone.
